Covid-19


(The data in the article and in the slides corresponds to data till May of 2021)

Ever since India witnessed the onset of Covid-19 pandemic in January 2020 it has taken the lives of 0.3 Million people till date. As India surfs through the second wave of the pandemic, the nation is facing a huge crisis owing to the lockdowns and protocols that obstruct the normal functioning of life. The only silver lining that lies ahead is to vaccinate the people effectively to weaken the effect of the corona virus on humans. It is indeed a huge challenge that lies ahead for the government to inoculate 1.38Billion people as they brace themselves for a possible third wave.
